This Acharya used to continually practice a high form of Yoga called Shiva Rajayoga and was hence known as Kaivalya Yogi.

From Punya Shloka Manjari

﻿8. आचार्य - 8 - कैवल्यानन्दयोगीन्द्रः
श्रीशैले ••• सन्ध्याहनि ॥ १६ ॥
Son of Śivayya of Śrīśaila, known as Manganna, held that position by his directions and became well-known as Kaivalya Yogi, ascended to the status of a Rājayogi and remaining (in that position) for eighty three years accomplished realisation in the evening of Makaraśaṅkarānti of the year Sarvadhāri.
