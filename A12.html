This Acharya, after appointing His successor at the Peetham and thereby arranging for its administration, went to an isolated place and took up a rare and great vow by name Sarvabhauma Charya. By this observance He won over fatigue, sleep and hunger.

From Punya Shloka Manjari

﻿12. आचार्य - 12 - श्रीचन्द्रशेखरेन्द्रयोगी
श्रीचिद्धनेन्द्रनियतः ••• सवपुस्तिरोऽधात् ॥ २३ ॥
आनन्द ••• पदमापत्परात्परम् ॥ २४ ॥
Initiated by Śrī Cidghanendra, the preceptor Śrī Candraśekhara, having adorned the Kāmakoṭi Pīṭha for sixty-three years, then having initiated/imparted on e named Saccidghana, vanished with form in the midst of disciples.
He attained his exalted Supreme state on the ninth day of the bright fortnight in the month of Āṣāḍha of the year Ānanda.
