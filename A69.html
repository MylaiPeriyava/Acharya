Shri Jayendra Saraswathi Shankaracharya (born Subramanyam Mahadeva; 18 July 1935 – 28 February 2018) was the 69th Shankaracharya Guru and head or pontiff of the Kanchi Kamakoti Peetham. Subramanyam Mahadeva Iyer was nominated by his predecessor, Shri Chandrashekarendra Saraswati, as his successor and was given the pontifical title Sri Jayendra Saraswathi on 22 March 1954. Shri Jayendra Saraswati Swamigal succeeded Shri Mahaperiyava Chandrashekara Saraswati Swamigal in 1960, a fact not many people know. Out of immense respect to his Guru, he continued leading all the activities of the mutt with the benevolent oversight of his Guru. His successor was anointed by the then Senior Pontiff Mahaperiyava during his lifetime in the early 1980s.
