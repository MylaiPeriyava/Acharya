﻿22. आचार्य - 22 - परिपूर्णबोधेन्द्रः
पुत्रो ••• सिद्धिमापत् ॥ ४७ ॥
Son of Rameśa Makhin, Madhura, a physician adept in the incatations of Grāvābhilāpaka, Paripūrṇabodha, the preceptor of Śrī Śārada Maṭha attained the beatitude on the night of bright fortnight of the month Kartika in the year Raudri.
