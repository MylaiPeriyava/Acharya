﻿24. आचार्य - 24 - च्चित्सुखेन्द्रः
सच्चित्सुखाच्छिव ••• पदं सः॥ ५० ॥
अपराभवो ••• नवमीश्वरद्वयम् ॥ ५१ ॥
Well-known as Śiva earlier, this Citsukhendra the governor (of the maṭh) remained in Koṅkaṇa; adorning the Preceptorship he was taking care of spiritual activities. He did not move a step from his place.
This revered Preceptor ever victorious, the self-restrained one, merged in the Supreme on the night in the month of Śravaṇa of the year Parābhava.
