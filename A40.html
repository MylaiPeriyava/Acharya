﻿40. आचार्य - 40 - महादेवेन्द्रसरस्वती
कर्णाटकन्नयसुतः ••• स्वधाम्नि ॥ ८३ ॥
अयमुज्ज्वल ••• स्पष्टम् ॥
Son of Kannayya of Karnataka, Śivarāmabhaṭṭa was placed in the pīṭha by Saccidvilāsayati; resplendent like the Sun, he adorned the pīṭha and merged in his effulgence on the sixth day of the bright fortnight of Vaiśākha in the year Bhava.
This Ujjvala Mahādevendra remained in the pīṭha for forty-two years and attained siddhi at Kāñci itself on the sixth day of Śuklapakṣa of Vaiśākha ,month in the year Bhava.
