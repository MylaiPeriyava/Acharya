﻿29. आचार्य - 29 - पूर्णबोधेन्द्रः
श्रीपतेस्तनयः ••• ईश्वरे ॥ ५८ ॥
अयमपि ••• सप्तदश (१७) वर्षाः ॥
Son of Śrīpati, known as Kṛṣṇa before initiation, the great sage Pūrṇabodhendra, merged in Brahman on the Ekādaśi (eleventh) day of the bright fortnight of the month Śravaṇa in the year Īśvara.
His preceptor-ship was for seventeen years.
