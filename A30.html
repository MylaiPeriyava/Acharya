﻿30 आचार्य - 30 - श्रीबालबोधेन्द्रः
कालहस्ति ••• चतुर्थ्यहर्मुखे ॥ ५९ ॥
अस्याधीश्यकालः ••• सिद्धिङ्गतः ॥
The preceptor Bālabodhendra was the son of Kālahasti, and was called Bālaya, bereft of ignorance, attained the Supreme state of immortality on the fourth day of the dark fortnight of the month of Vaiśāka in the year Ānanda.
His preceptor-ship was for thirty-seven years. He attained siddhi in Kāñci.
