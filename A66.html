From Punya Shloka Manjari

﻿66. आचार्य - 66 - चन्द्रशेखरेन्द्रः
Preceptor Śrī Candraśekhara adorned the pīṭha for seventeen years. He reached his eternal beatitude on the eighth day of the black fortnight in the month of Māgha of the year Parābhava.
The name of this preceptor before initiation was Svāminātha. Parents were well-known Maṅgalāmbikā and Sītārāmaśāstri. His place of siddhi was village Kalavai in Śalivahana era 1830.
